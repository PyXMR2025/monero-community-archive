# Original Description
Diego is hosting this Coffee Chat.

---

![Monero Coffee Chat](https://i.imgur.com/7G4Powo.png)

**Introduction**

This monthly Monero Coffee Chat meeting will feature longtime active members of the Monero community, who will answer questions from others. It will be loosely organized, and it will run about one hour.

This meeting hopes to make the community more accessible and open. It will also serve as a medium to communicate the latest and upcoming developments.

**Location**

**YouTube Livestream** with Jitsi, a free open-source video conferencing tool.

**Time**

The chat is at approximately **17:00 UTC.**

**Proposed Agenda**

Monero Coffee Chats intend to be casual and encourage free flows of thought. Nevertheless, the meeting will be broadly guided by the following topics depending on attendance:

How people can contribute
Current initiatives and progress
Future developments
Research lab "office hours"
Guiding philosophies
