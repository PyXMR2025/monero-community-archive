# Original Description
I was scanning through the codebase and observed use of "grandfathering" in  https://github.com/monero-project/monero/blob/master/src/cryptonote_core/blockchain.cpp. 

I propose we replace its use with more inclusive terms like "legacied" or "exempted".

For context on the complicated nature of the term and its racist origin please refer to: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grandfather_clause
