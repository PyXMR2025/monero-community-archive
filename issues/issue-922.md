# Original Description
For the sake of clarity, I am stepping down as a member of the Monero Core Team effective immediately. I have not really had any active role in the Core Team in many years, and this is in line with my stated goals since 2018.

![Screenshot 2023-11-06 at 10 11 02 AM](https://github.com/monero-project/meta/assets/1944293/a5f7ff00-0e8e-48b5-b2b8-7ab2561a6a7c)

As part of this, any remaining privileges or access I have will be handed over or revoked. It is my hope that it can be [handed over to new workgroups](https://github.com/monero-project/meta/issues/921) in the coming weeks / months, instead of the Core Team continuing in a highly centralised model.
