## Riven-Spell | 2021-03-12T09:27:45+00:00
Hey there, Patrick!

I wouldn't recommend trying to mine XMR on your GPU. It's not exactly profitable, and the RandomX algorithm is optimized for CPUs. You'd be best off mining against GPU pools that can pay in XMR.

Even the best GPUs offer a mere fraction of what a good CPU can pull (but that's the intent, to strive for 1 CPU = 1 vote).
