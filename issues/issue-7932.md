# Original Description
After greetings , This is an urgent required feature to allow monero's adoption in white markets , 

The view key feature of the cryptonote whitepaper was intended to add a regulatory compliance feature , but it wasn't implemented well , as you can't select certain transactions from history to show , either all of the transactions history or none , and this isn't suitable for multi national businesses , as each country local regulations differ , hence the selective transactions view key is the solution , also this feature will open a new business domain based on view keys smart contracts "a hint from the ETH community" , also these smart contracts can be tied to the digital signatures certificate authorities " a hint from me" , 
Finally I believe that this feature will ease the lack of monero's mass adoption  in regulated businesses , while opening new business domains based on selective centralized transactions , 

## you don't need to use multiple wallets , you can just reveal the required transactions from your unified monero official wallet.
