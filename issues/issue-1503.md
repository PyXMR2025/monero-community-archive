# Original Description
As discussed in #1483, the MONERO header inthe homepage is redundant and that valuable space could be used for something with more impact on the visitor.

I think we have three options:

1. Replace the MONERO header with something else and keep 'A private DigitalCurrency' under it
2. Remove both headers and replace them with a 2 different headers.
3. Remove both headers and replaced with 1 single header

What of the three options do you prefer?
What headers(s) should we use?
