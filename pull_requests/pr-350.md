## QuickBASIC | 2017-08-17T19:48:09+00:00
> unbreakable cryptography

Debatable, but no cryptography is unbreakable.

> obfuscated blockchain

The blockchain itself is not obfuscated, it is visible and verifiable to everyone. (Even if the verifier can't know the amounts unless they have the private key for the transaction, they can validate that transactions are valid and not double spends.) It would be more accurate to say that the signer of a transaction is hidden and the amount is obfuscated.
