- Prevent project abandonment
- Guarantee that endor had encouragement/help with the uncompleted MS2 + their part of MS3

mj however, has not only completed their part of MS3 but also MS4. [Endor states:](https://repo.getmonero.org/monero-project/ccs-proposals/-/merge_requests/299#note_19778)

> I confirm that I can complete the remainder of my work on M2+M3+M4 independently 
(though of course any collaboration is always welcome), and that mj can be paid for his part of M3+M4 
(20.10 XMR + 20.10 XMR, as per the proposal) right away.
>
> -- <cite>endor</cite>


There has been a public controversy surrounding this proposal. 
The CCS itself is devoid of opinion/emotion regarding 'extra curricular' activities. Work done as per your proposal is work paid. Currently, SoloptXMR:
- Contains no dev tax.
- With initial setup effort from the end user, external readings via sensors/cameras will control a mining rig. 

Now that mj has completed their part of MS3+4 / endor requires no further help / their working relationship is over - 
reasons for withholding MS3 (and now MS4) may need to be reevaluated.
