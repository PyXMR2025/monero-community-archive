## Justxd22 | 2022-01-26T17:11:07+00:00
For anyone asking same question,
Colors are current difficulty indicator
Red - means current block diff exceed 100% (more effort is being done which is normal doesn't affect payment)
Green - means normal effort zone less than 100%

COLORS HAVE NOTHING TO DO WITH HIGH PING IT'S NOT RELATED (i taught I'm answering jobs sent late so it's red which is false)
