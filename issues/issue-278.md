# Original Description
![Monero Coffee Chat](https://i.imgur.com/7G4Powo.png)

**Introduction**

This monthly meeting will feature longtime active members of the Monero community, who will answer questions from others. It will be loosely organized, and it will run about one hour.

This meeting hopes to make the community more accessible and open. It will also serve as a medium to communicate the latest and upcoming developments.

Diego has stepped up to lead this one, since I will not be free unfortunately.

**Location**

**[YouTube Livestream](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tD9HMx-i2Zk)** with Jitsi, a free open-source video conferencing tool.

**Time**

The meeting is at **16:00 UTC.** This is an hour earlier than the standard 17:00 UTC time to make room for #277.

**Proposed Agenda**

Monero Coffee Chats intend to be casual and encourage free flows of thought. Nevertheless, the meeting will be guided by the following topics:

How people can contribute
Current initiatives and progress
Future developments
Research lab "office hours"
Guiding philosophies
