I just had a literal showerthought. At the moment the team page is not very useful if somebody is looking for info about who to contact. I think the sections *Community* and *Developers* could be changed, in *Coordinators* and *Hall of fame*.

The *Coordinators* page would list all the coordinators of all the workgroups, with links and contacts, so that a visiotr immediatly know who to ask info to or where to look for more details.

The *Hall of Fame* should be a unic page including both *Community* and *Developers*. Basically the list of "individuals that have gone above and beyond for Monero". We could use this to push contributions or as a reward for particularly involved contributors, i don't know, just throw your ideas.

I think this way the *Team* page could be much more useful, right now it's basically just a list of names. Opinions about this?
