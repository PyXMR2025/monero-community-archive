## hyc | 2022-08-15T15:31:38+00:00
If you're copying something in DB order, in this case you could use both MDB_APPEND and MDB_APPENDDUP. Their effects are independent, MDB_APPEND applies to the primary key, MDB_APPENDDUP applies only to the dupsort values.

Of course MDB_APPEND may be irrelevant if the primary key is just the fake zerokval.
