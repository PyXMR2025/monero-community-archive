An F.A.Q. (FAQ read as an acronym)

## ndorf | 2018-10-18T16:09:22+00:00
SSD is an initialism, not an acronym. Acronyms are pronounced like words (like "a FAQ"). Initialisms are spelled out (like "an SSD"). :)
