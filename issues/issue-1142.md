## moneroguides | 2022-05-07T16:27:56+00:00
"It is possible to derive new and unique key pairs, also known as sub-addresses, from your primary public/private key pair.

Sub-addresses were introduced to the Monero ecosystem to enhance the privacy of its users. 

Users are able to create many sub-addresses and benefit from this feature when they give all new payers a different sub-address.

Users of sub-addresses should be aware of the [janus attack](https://web.getmonero.org/2019/10/18/subaddress-janus.html) when trying to maintain anonymity."
