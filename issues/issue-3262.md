## lh1008 | 2018-09-05T16:15:31+00:00
Hi @jeandudey 

The Spanish community has several communication channels where we can meet or just hang out. There is the IRC #monero-es or [mattermost chat #monero-es](https://mattermost.getmonero.org/monero/channels/monero-es), where we can coordinate Spanish work through the community, find support, or just help the Spanish community grow. We also have a telegram chat room [@MoneroEsp](https://t.me/MoneroEsp) where you can also hang out, ask anything, find support or meet the monero-es community.

The [Kovri](https://getkovri.org/) website is also being translated and is being reviewed. You could help there if you want. [Update Spanish i18n #88](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://github.com/monero-project/kovri-docs/pull/88%23issuecomment-414160000&source=gmail&ust=1536249112499000&usg=AFQjCNFCBLLJxf8dGLVHt1b9i-G42yr44Q)
