## moneroguides | 2022-05-07T16:37:11+00:00
"Change is one of three outputs produced during every transaction.

It is the remainder after both the desired transaction amount and transaction fee have been satisfied.

In the case that the whole amount is used for the output and transaction fee, change is still generated with a value of 0. This is to make it indistinguishable from any other transaction on the blockchain."
