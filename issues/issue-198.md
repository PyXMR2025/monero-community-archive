# Original Description
# Press and Media

**Reporters and journalists** ask general questions about Monero, like _'What makes Monero different than Bitcoin?', 'Why do unsavory people like Monero so much?', or 'What is a Monero ICO?'_ We need a page of good answers to common press questions, like a Press FAQ.

## New Document

The **Press FAQ** should be a one to two hour project, contributed by the most knowledgeable community members. It should:

- [ ] Be printed on one A4 sized paper
- [ ] Contain answers to ten to twenty questions
- [ ] Be updated on a quarterly basis (for hardforks and events)
- [ ] Be stored in a easy to find place (monero-project/meta/press/)
