## jeffro256 | 2026-04-10T16:47:11+00:00
This doesn't handle varint containers very well. For example, in your unit test, if you change `v` to a `std::vector<std::uint64_t>`, then the serialization will work because then `use_container_varint()` is `true`. A varint container, even though `sizeof()` may be 8, may legitimately have as many elements as there are bytes left. In other words, its "compression ratio" may be 8.
