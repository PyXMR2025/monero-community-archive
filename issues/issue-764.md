# Original Description
![Monero Meet](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/12520755/99100229-2bd0d200-25a1-11eb-854b-4ff3bcd2a14c.jpg)

Hello everyone!

It's been a long time since the last Monero Meet in January, and even longer since I hosted them regularly (over 2 years ago). But, they're back!

Channel: https://www.youtube.com/@MoneroSpaceWorkgroup

The first one will be at 1pm CT on Tuesday, December 20th. They will occur every 4 weeks, rain or shine!

Monero Meets are somewhat casual video calls with Monero community members to discuss closely-related Monero topics. It generally falls somewhere between Monero Talk and Monerotopia for casualness. I feel that a void was left, and these will be useful for building camaraderie.

Monero Meets are a great way to stay up to date on the latest news and have friendly, insightful conversations with many Monero community members. The first similar "Monero Coffee Chat" conversation happened all the back back in October 2017.

It will be a livestream with an uploaded podcast later. We will use VDO ninja.

I'll place these videos on the Monero Meet channel so it has its own identity.

If you are interested in joining, you can message me:

* Matrix: @sgp:magicgrants.org
* Signal: 708-252-3261
* Email: justin@monero.ceo 😛

**Proposed Agenda**

Monero Meets intend to be casual and encourage free flows of thought. Nevertheless, the meeting will be broadly guided by the following topics depending on attendance:

Recent Monero news
How people can contribute
Current initiatives and progress
Future developments
Research lab "office hours"
Guiding philosophies
