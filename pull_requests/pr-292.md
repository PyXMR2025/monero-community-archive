### What
Enables and fixes workspace lints for `cuprate-levin`.

# Discussion History
## hinto-janai | 2024-09-18T21:47:01+00:00
Tests being laid out like they are in `consensus/`, `net/`, `p2p/` is pretty painful for the unused dependency lint since `tests/` can't be detected. 

For now I've done this and `#![expect(clippy::tests_outside_test_module, unused_crate_dependencies)]` in the `tests/` files for now but surely there's a better solution that isn't removing the lint.
