## binaryFate | 2019-06-08T09:39:52+00:00
Hello @SamsungGalaxyPlayer,

I have an additional agenda item:

[OSTIF](https://ostif.org/) has kindly offered to be an intermediary between the CCS funds and the firms expecting USD for the RandomX audit. Derek Zimmer took care of this in practice.
Due to volatility, the 1293.8 XMR transferred to OSTIF resulted in a left over of ~4500$, additionally to what is required to pay the 3 firms, and pay for the international wire transfers.
There is no defined rule for CCS funds leftover in the form of USD. 
Some potential options:

* Convert them back to XMR, so they can be sent to the general fund. We may take a hit depending on the price change

* A donation to OSTIF, besides helping us the goals of this organization are quite aligned with Monero

* Derek potential suggestion: a donation on behalf of Monero/OSTIF to something clearly privacy oriented in need of funding like https://blog.torproject.org/how-use-meek-pluggable-transport

Because of the lack of clearly defined rules in this case, I would like the options to be discussed in public and if possible reach a decision at the meeting.
