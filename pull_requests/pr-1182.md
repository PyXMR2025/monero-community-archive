## Jaqueeee | 2018-03-15T20:18:43+00:00
It's related to #387. If the difference between currentBlock and targetBlock is small, the progress bar would be close to 100% filled during the whole sync progress. 

blocksToSync is set at wallet startup and the progress bar indicated the sync progress from 0% to 100% in the current session. If you restart the wallet, the bar restarts from 0%.
