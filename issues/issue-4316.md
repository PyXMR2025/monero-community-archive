# Original Description
Proposal to implement Atomic Swaps directly from the app that automatically picks the best deal without having to visit a 3rd party websites to do them.

## Why?
1. Quality of Life Improvement
2. Reduces the risk of providers artificially increasing the fees for swaps
3. Security through avoiding trackers and JS from these websites and lesser risk of scams
4. Privacy by avoiding cloudflare-gates etc..
