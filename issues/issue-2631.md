## ronohara | 2017-10-28T04:57:39+00:00
#1073 is right - threads required.   I added 'threads' to the USE variable in /etc/portage/make.conf (system wide) and emerged everything to ensure that thread support is enabled in all possible places.

emerge -uDvN --keep-going world
