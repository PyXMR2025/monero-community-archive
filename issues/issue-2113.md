# Original Description
Idea to add a 1% donation to the Monero Core Team general fund that can be optionally increased through config or removed by changing the source code.

At current issuance rates and Monero prices, this is approximately $650,000 / year if the total Monero hashrate donated. Even with lower % hashrate commitment, this is enough to fund the Monero Research Lab. Some MRL full-timers express significant stress in opening a CCS for donations every several months. This donation would give the MRL full-timers the fund cushion they need to feel job-secure while also dispersing the costs widely and optionally.
