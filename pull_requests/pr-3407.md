## leonklingele | 2018-03-15T06:56:50+00:00
Constructing translations by concatenating sentence parts might work in some languages, however presumes a grammar and defined sentence structure.
In German for example, the proper translation would be `$AMOUNT XMR werden an das Monero Projekt ($URL oder $ADDR) gespendet.` which has a completely different structure. (BTW: `XMR` is missing in the English text)

Rather than fixing the old and broken scheme, I suggest using formatted strings instead:

`sprintf(buf, "Donating %s XMR to the Monero Project (%s or %s).", amount_str, MONERO_DONATION_URL, MONERO_DONATION_ADDR);`

## stoffu | 2018-03-15T07:43:08+00:00
Good point. I’d suggest the use of `boost::format` which is commonly used in the codebase.
