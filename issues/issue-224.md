## bitlamas | 2018-05-26T01:47:57+00:00
The first mention about the possibility of reallocating the funds was raised in the last community meeting, or the one before. Can't remember. It was firstly raised because we were raising funds for both the Bulletproofs audit and the Advancing Monero Hardware Wallet -- and someone asked if we couldn't reallocate the XMR from partially funded ideas to these other ones.

One example is the Monero Tracking Challenge 2, which has raised 260 XMR from the requested 500 XMR. There's a strong belief that this "old idea" won't get more funding and that it somewhat overlaps with the current program that Monero Project has on HackerOne.

We're in the initial stages of discussing what could be done in such cases.
