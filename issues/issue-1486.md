# Original Description
Monero does not have an obvious place for press and conference organizers to contact members of the community. I strongly recommend revise the "Press Kit" page.

First, change it to the name "Press Info and Contact" or similar

Then, place a large driving point at the top. Make the contact email extremely obvious. Add images showing Monero at conferences (I received permission from Sunita to use). At the bottom, we can continue with the current cards.

The current Monero logo card is absolutely terrible. No one ever needs "small" or "medium" sizes. Also, no one ever needs the "no background" versions. We should only provide the "white background" versions of both in large PNG and AI. Everything else wastes valuable space and causes harmful confusion.

I don't love the remaining current cards, but I'd rather leave as-is to speed things up.
