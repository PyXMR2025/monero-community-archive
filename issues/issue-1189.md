# Original Description
I am looking at the homepage, wanting to find the "merchants" page to share with someone. Only I can't see the option because I instinctively looked under "Get Started" for information on how to spend monero. 

If I was new to monero then I would not consider myself part of the "community" so I would not look there for information about the merchants. Also, I think many stores accepting monero are not deep parts of the community on an indivdiual level. 

Therefore I propose that the menu item "Spending" is added to the "Get started" page, just underneath "accepting".
