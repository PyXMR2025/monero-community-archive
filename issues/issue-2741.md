# Original Description
When making a transaction some of your funds will be locked. If you go to Account you can view your 'Total balance' and 'Total unlocked balance'. The issue is that when your funds do unlock, your 'Total unlocked balance' will not be updated. You have to reload the Account tab to see the current state.

This happens on Carbon Chameleon and also happened on Boron Butterfly.
