# Original Description
History page rewritten.

![](https://i.imgur.com/5Yhrj5S.png)

Changes to what we currently have:

- Now called the 'transactions' page.
- Paginated results - works well for wallets with a lot of transactions (tested on wallet with 3700 txs).
  - Functionality to jump to a specific page in the pagination menu.
  - Pagination also works over filtered results.
- Configurable date display format: 'ago string' and 'YYYY-MM-hh:mm:ss'
- Blockheight now correctly reports 'transaction pool' when a TX in transaction pool
- When the transaction history changes due to incoming/outgoing funds, or simply needing to update the confirmations due to new blocks, the data will update, yet active filters and pagination will remain unchanged.

I did my best to create a pleasant UX experience. Something that is robust and just works. I like how I can quickly establish what my biggest transactions were, my oldest ones, or what I did last week in terms of transaction history. Now, this was already (mostly) possible with our old UX, however, this is nicer to operate and in addition adds new functionality. Try it out and let me know what you think.

Reference design: [Img](https://github.com/GBKS/monero-wallet-design/blob/master/screens/desktop-dark/transactions.png)
